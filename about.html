Field Notes

EcoGIS Studio focuses on understanding ecological systems through spatial analysis and mapping. My work integrates wildlife ecology, landscape patterns, and conservation research to explore how environments interact and change over time.

Observations are drawn from both field-based awareness and geospatial analysis.

Approach

I combine field observation, spatial data analysis, and GIS modeling to interpret ecological patterns across different scales.
