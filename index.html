EcoGIS Studio

Mapping wildlife, landscapes, and ecological systems

Field Overview

EcoGIS Studio brings together wildlife ecology, spatial analysis, and visual storytelling through GIS-based projects and field observation. The work focuses on understanding how animals, landscapes, and human infrastructure interact across space and time.

This portfolio includes spatial analysis projects, interactive StoryMaps, nature photography, and web-based tools designed to communicate ecological patterns in accessible and visual ways.

FIELD NOTE 01

Landscape Connectivity

Spatial analysis can reveal how fragmented environments influence movement, habitat accessibility, and ecological resilience across landscapes.
